Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Escape to the beauty and serenity of the California North Coast at Pacific Trails Resort. Our luxury camping yurts and upscale lodge offer the perfect getaway for couples who love nature and hiking.

Yurt Accommodations

Experience the ultimate luxury camping experience in our spacious yurts. Each yurt features a comfortable queen-sized bed, wood-burning stove, and private deck with stunning views of the surrounding forest.

Activities

  • Hiking
  • Wildlife watching
  • Kayaking
  • Birdwatching
  • Stargazing

With miles of trails to explore, the California North Coast offers endless opportunities for outdoor recreation. At Pacific Trails Resort, you can hike through ancient redwood forests, paddle along the coast in a kayak, and watch for otters and seals playing in the surf. In the evening, sit by the campfire and gaze up at the stars.
